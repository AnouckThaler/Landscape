MusiTalents

Our Company

We are a plateform dedicated to new artists, making it possible for them to create, share and monetize their music whilst still being able to connect with their public. MusiTalents proposes tools of music production collaborative, advice on their career management and participative finance tools to support their musical projects. It's objective is to help independant artists the means to develop their career without going through the "normal route".
